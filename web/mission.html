Missions


The mission of the Agriculture Department in India is to promote sustainable agricultural growth and ensure food security for the nation by supporting farmers with modern technologies, quality inputs, and effective policies. It aims to enhance productivity, profitability, and competitiveness of Indian agriculture while safeguarding natural resources and promoting eco-friendly practices. The department is committed to empowering farmers through education, capacity-building, and access to credit and markets, ensuring their socio-economic development.
