Prakritik Kheti Kushhal Kisan Yojana

Prakritik Kheti Khushhal Kisan Yojana is an important scheme launched by the Government of Himachal Pradesh to promote natural (organic) farming among farmers. The aim of this scheme is to encourage chemical-free, low-cost, and eco-friendly farming practices, ensuring better health for consumers and higher profits for farmers.

Key Features of Prakritik Kheti Kushhal Kisan Yojana:
  • reduce the use of chemical fertilizers and pesticides in farming.
  • promote natural farming methods that improve soil health and fertility.
  • To help farmers reduce the cost of cultivation and increase income.
  • To protect the environment and water sources from harmful chemicals.
  • To produce healthy and chemical-free food for consumers.

Benefits
  • Improves soil health and fertility over time.
  • Reduces farming costs as it avoids expensive chemical inputs.
  • Produces healthy, organic crops with good market demand.
  • Protects environment from chemical pollution.
  • Ensures better income for farmers through organic produce.
