Famrming Methods and Instruction


Farming Methods for Better Vegetation :

  1. Use of crop rotation to maintain soil fertility.
  2. Practice organic farming to avoid harmful chemicals.
  3. Adopt drip irrigation and sprinkler systems for efficient water use.
  4. Use bio-fertilizers and compost to enrich the soil naturally.
  5. Select high-quality and disease-resistant seeds.
  6. Apply mulching to retain soil moisture.
  7. Perform timely sowing and harvesting of crops.

Precautions Farmers Should Take :

  • Avoid overuse of chemical fertilizers and pesticides.
  • Ensure proper spacing between plants for healthy growth.
  • Monitor crops regularly for pests and diseases.
  • Use clean and adequate water for irrigation.
  • Protect crops from extreme weather using proper techniques like shelters or covers.
  • Regularly test soil quality to know nutrient needs.
