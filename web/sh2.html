Rajya Krishi Yantrikaran Program

The State Agricultural Mechanization Program is an important initiative launched by the government to provide modern agricultural machines and equipment to farmers at subsidized rates. The aim of this program is to promote the use of advanced technology in farming to increase productivity, reduce labor costs, and make agriculture more efficient and profitable.

Key Features of Rajya Krishi Yantrikaran Program:
  • To provide financial assistance (subsidy) for the purchase of advanced agricultural machinery.
  • To make modern tools and equipment accessible to small and marginal farmers.
  • To reduce the cost of cultivation and save time in farming activities.
  • To promote mechanization in agriculture for better output and efficiency.
  • To make farming easier, cost-effective, and productive.

Benefits
  • Subsidy on machines like tractors, rotavators, seed drills, threshers, harvesters, etc.
  • Helps increase crop productivity and farmers' income.
  • Enables farmers to complete work in less time with less effort.
  • Solves the problem of labor shortage during peak farming seasons.
